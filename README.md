# TravellersInfinityStones
A classic rounds mod updated and brought back to life by a new developer
# About
Collect all Six infinity stones, collecting each stone puts your life on a timer starting at 40 seconds and decreasing by 4 each stone.
Collecting all Six stones will grant a huge stat bonus and give you the one time ability to wipe all players for that round
And remember, With Great Power, Comes Great Responsiblity
